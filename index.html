Frequently Asked Questions

Yes. Tickets are fully refundable up to 14 days before the event.

Standard tickets include 30 days of digital recordings. Pro tickets include lifetime access.

DevConf 2026 takes place at the Metropolitan Convention Center.

Can students attend?

Yes, students get 50% off Standard tickets with a valid student ID at check-in.

What should I bring?

Just your laptop and badge. Chargers and swag will be waiting at registration.
